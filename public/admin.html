☕ Restaurants

▦ Bars & cafés

The smaller cards in the “Bars & Cafés” section of your Dining page. Edit any card, or add a new one with its own photo.

▦ Activities timetable

The “Your week, day by day” grid on your Activities page. Tap a day to edit what’s on (e.g. Morning → “Beach yoga · 08:00”). The seven days are fixed — you edit them, rather than adding or removing days. A label of “Evening” or “Night” is highlighted on the website.

▦ Filter categories

The filter pills above your Gallery (Beach, Pools, …). Add a new one — e.g. “Facilities” — then tag photos to it below. “All” is always shown automatically and isn’t listed here.

▦ Photos

Every photo on your Gallery page. Edit a photo’s caption, change which category it’s filed under, or replace its picture — and add brand-new photos. A photo with no picture shows a “Photo coming soon” placeholder.

German & Russian wording

Your website can be shown in English, German or Russian. Whenever you add or change wording in the portal, its German and Russian versions are added here — fill them in so visitors who pick those languages see the whole site translated, not half-English. Anything still missing a translation is highlighted. Leave a box blank and that item simply stays in English for that language.

? How this works

A quick guide for getting comfortable.

  1. Pick a section on the left — Contact, Homepage, Rooms, News or Images.
  2. Make your change by typing in a box or uploading a photo. Your changes save automatically as a draft.
  3. Press "Publish changes" at the top right to make them appear on the live website.
  4. Press "Preview site" any time to open your website and check how it looks.
  5. Download a backup regularly from the Backup section, just to be safe.
Going live: right now your login and content live in this browser for the demo. When your developer connects the SQL Server database, your sign-in becomes secure and encrypted, and your content is stored centrally so it shows for every visitor. The way you edit here will not change.

⌕ Search engine listing (SEO)

This is what Google shows for each page — the blue clickable title and the grey description underneath. It updates automatically from your content every time you press Publish changes: edit a room, a price or your contact details and the search listing follows. You don't have to do anything here. But if you want to word a page's title or description differently for Google, type your own version below — leave a box empty to use the automatic one.
